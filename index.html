Green Zones, Orange Zones and Red Zones

Green Zone: Areas with zero confirmed cases till date or no confirmed case in the last 21 days.
Orange Zone: Areas which have reported a limited number of cases in the past and no surge in positive cases recently.
Red Zone: Areas or the hotspots classified as those with the highest caseload.

How COVID 19 SPREADS

People can catch COVID-19 from others who have the virus.
The disease can spread from person to person through small
droplets from the nose or mouth which are spread when a person
with COVID-19 coughs or exhales. These droplets land on objects
and surfaces around the person.

Symtoms of the Corona Virus
